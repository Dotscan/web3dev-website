About Web3Dev

We are the most open, collaborative, passionate group of free thinkers, boundary pushers, creators, builders, innovators, and dreamers

Founded in 2020, Web3Dev is the world's leading blockchain developer support platform. We believe that developers are the core force driving the Web3 revolution, and we are committed to providing developers with the highest quality educational resources, development tools, and community support.

Our vision is to create a borderless developer ecosystem where global innovators can collaborate freely to build the decentralized future together. By lowering technical barriers, providing learning resources, and building community platforms, we are shaping the developer culture of the Web3 era.

Share your ideas, projects, and vision for an open web. We are a passionate community, eager to meet you and push this industry forward together.
